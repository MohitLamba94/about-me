Projects

Restoring images captured in Night

We all love to take photographs, but low-light conditions such as night-time, pose a significant challenge in capturing good photographs. Common solutions such as flashlight, NIR and large exposure timings tend to cause artifacts in the captured images and may not even be available. The works listed below aim at restoring night-time images but without any additional hardware manipulation.

Digital Forensics

Digital media is gradually replacing printed documents in courts, banks, academic institutions, etc and have already become an integral part of our lives via smartphones, Twitter, Facebook, etc. But editing these digital images is now a lot easier and is easily achieved in no time using photo editing softwares such as Adobe Photoshop, GIMP, etc. In this project, I aim to detect the genuiness of digital images and identify potential tampering with the original data.
