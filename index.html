Projects

Restoring images captured in Night

We all love to take photographs, but low-light conditions such as night-time, pose a significant challenge in capturing good photographs. Common solutions such as flashlight, NIR and large exposure timings tend to cause artifacts in the captured images and may not even be available. The works listed below aim at restoring night-time images using normal DSLR cameras without any additional hardware cost or manipulation.

Digital Forensics

Digital media is gradually replacing printed documents in courts, banks, academic institutions, etc and have already become an integral part of our lives via smartphones, Twitter, Facebook, etc. But tampering such digital images is now a lot easier and is easily achieved in no time using photo editing software such as Adobe Photoshop, GIMP, etc. In this project, I aim to detect digital images' genuineness and identify and localize potential tampering to prevent evidence falsification.
